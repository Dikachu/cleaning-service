Privacy Policy

At Cleaning Agent, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This comprehensive Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our professional cleaning services or visit our website.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and address for service delivery
  • Service Information: Cleaning service preferences, property details, and special requirements
  • Communication Records: Emails, phone calls, and other communications with our cleaning team
  • Payment Information: Billing details and payment history (processed securely through our payment partners)
  • Property Access: Entry codes, keys, or access information for cleaning services

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our professional cleaning services
  • Communicate with you about appointments and cleaning schedules
  • Process payments and send invoices for cleaning services
  • Send service updates and promotional offers (with your consent)
  • Ensure the safety and security of our cleaning team and your property
  • Comply with legal obligations and industry regulations
  • Maintain service quality and customer satisfaction

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • With your explicit consent for specific purposes
  • To trusted service providers who assist us in operating our cleaning business
  • When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • To emergency services if required for safety reasons

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our security protocols include:

  • Encrypted data transmission and storage
  • Secure access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Employee training on data protection

Note: While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

As a customer, you have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • File a complaint with relevant authorities
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Terms of Service

By using Cleaning Agent's professional cleaning services, you agree to these comprehensive terms and conditions. Please read them carefully before booking our services.

Service Agreement

When you book our cleaning services, you enter into a service agreement with Cleaning Agent. We agree to provide professional cleaning services according to the specifications outlined in your quote and service plan. This agreement covers:

  • Service scope and specifications
  • Pricing and payment terms
  • Service scheduling and access requirements
  • Quality standards and guarantees

Booking and Cancellation Policy

Our flexible booking and cancellation policy ensures convenience for our customers:

  • Advance Booking: Services must be booked at least 24 hours in advance
  • Free Cancellation: Free cancellation up to 4 hours before scheduled service
  • Rescheduling: Changes can be made up to 2 hours before service time
  • Late Cancellation: Cancellations within 4 hours may incur a 25% fee
  • Emergency Cancellations: Special consideration for emergencies

Service Requirements

To ensure optimal service delivery and safety, we require:

  • Access to all areas requiring cleaning
  • Removal of valuable items and personal belongings
  • Pets to be secured in a separate area
  • Clear pathways and work areas
  • Proper lighting and ventilation
  • Notification of any special requirements or concerns

Service Guarantee

We stand behind our work with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with our cleaning service:

  • Contact us within 24 hours of service completion
  • We will return to address any concerns at no additional cost
  • If issues persist, we offer a full refund for the service
  • Documentation of concerns may be required

Liability and Insurance

Cleaning Agent maintains comprehensive liability insurance and bonding for your protection. However, we are not responsible for:

  • Damage to items not properly secured or removed
  • Pre-existing damage or wear and tear
  • Issues arising from improper access or unsafe conditions
  • Delays due to circumstances beyond our control
  • Damage to items of extraordinary value

Payment Terms and Methods

We offer flexible payment options for your convenience:

  • Payment Methods: We accept cash, credit cards, bank transfers, and digital payments
  • Payment Due: Payment is due upon completion of services
  • Late Payment: Late payments may incur additional fees
  • Refunds: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Recurring Services: Monthly billing available for regular customers

Professional Code of Conduct

Our team members are trained professionals who adhere to strict standards:

  • Arrive on time and in professional attire
  • Treat your property with respect and care
  • Maintain confidentiality of your personal information
  • Follow all safety protocols and best practices
  • Report any issues or concerns immediately
  • Use eco-friendly cleaning products when possible
  • Maintain professional communication at all times
